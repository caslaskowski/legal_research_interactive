Chapter 8 · Practice

The ADA Canvas

One legal question rarely lives in one source. Here the ADA’s public-accommodation rules are a connected map: pull a statute onto the canvas, click a cross-reference or a defined term, and watch the regulation and the cases that answer it appear beside it — edges and all.

How it works. Pick a starter question below to place your first card. Inside any card, click a dashed link — a section cross-reference or a defined term — to open that source next to it, connected by an edge. Drag cards by their header to arrange your map; drag the background to pan. The chips under the question track the sources a careful researcher would open; find them all to unlock the discussion.

Case text: public-domain court opinions, excerpted; the notes beneath them were written for this exercise. Authorities verified July 2026 — the law moves; run the method, don’t trust the module.
