Right Tool, Right Question

A generative tool is one instrument among several — and the choice is driven by the same thing that has driven every choice in this book: the question you are holding. Ten tasks below. For each, pick the tool you would actually reach for. Several have a defensible second choice; the reasoning for every option is shown once you answer.

How to use this: pick, then read why each option works or doesn’t. Where the answer is “AI, then verify,” the verification is not a formality — it is the four-step framework’s second step, and skipping it is how sanctions happen.
