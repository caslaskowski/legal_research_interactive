Weigh the Agency Materials

Seven situations, each turning on which agency material controls — or merely persuades. The recurring move: name what kind of document you are holding before you tell anyone what it requires.

How to use this: make the call, then read the reasoning under every option — each one either confirms the distinction or points to the panel worth rereading. All scenarios are fictional.
