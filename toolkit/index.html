Toolkit & Resources

The book’s working tools, gathered in one place: the Research Log, the checklists that guard each stage of the cycle, and the quick references worth keeping beside every research task. Each one opens for reading here and downloads as plain text you can fill in, print, or paste into your own documents.

How to use this: open any tool to read it; use its download button to save a fill-in copy. Downloads are generated entirely in your browser — nothing is sent or stored anywhere.
