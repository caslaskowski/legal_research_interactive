Glossary Flashcards

Every entry in the book's glossary, plus the key terms Chapters 9–16 define in their text — as one deck. Terms are defined here as the book uses them; the fuller treatment lives at each term's first appearance in the chapters.

How to use this: flip the card, then be honest — Got it retires the card for this pass, Again sends it to the back of the pile. Your progress saves in this browser automatically. Download a progress file before you leave a shared machine, and re-load it anywhere.
