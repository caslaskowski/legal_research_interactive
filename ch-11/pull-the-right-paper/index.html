Pull the Right Paper

Every document on a docket answers a different question. The complaint carries allegations; the memo carries arguments; the order carries what the court did. Eight research needs below — pull the paper that answers each, and remember the chapter's first discipline: court documents are a research tool, not a source of authority.

How to use this: pick the document, then read why each option does or doesn't fit. All scenarios are fictional.
